Who Is Cross Technology?


My name is Barry Bradshaw and I am the founder of Cross Technology.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from DeVry in 1997 and started my IT career with National Engineering Technology as a software engineer. NET is a firm that specializes in Intelligent Transportation Systems. While with NET I worked with many clients including the Minnesota Department of Transportation, Illinois Department of Transportation, and the Port Authority of New York & New Jersey. 

In 1999 I went to work for Distribution 2000, Inc., a third-party logistics provider based in Bolingbrook, IL as their Information Systems Manager. During my employment with Distribution 2000 I rewrote and enhanced a major portion of their WMS system. I replaced the RF system and rewrote the wireless client programs used by the system. I also migrated the backend database for the WMS from Oracle to MySQL.

In 2007 I decided to step out on my own and as a result formed Cross Technology.

Mission Statement

My mission through Cross Technology is to glorify God by serving others using the gifts that our heavenly Father has blessed me with. I will service my clientele with an ethical standard second to none.

By doing my best to model the servant nature of my Savior I hope to draw those around me close to the Lord where they can begin their own personal relationships with Him.
